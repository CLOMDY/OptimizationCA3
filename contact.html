Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take? +

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A simple website takes 4–6 weeks, while a complex web application can take 3–6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch? +

Yes! We offer various support and maintenance packages ranging from basic bug fixes to full ongoing development partnerships. 98% of our clients continue working with us after their initial project.

What is your payment structure? +

We typically work with a 40% upfront deposit, 40% at project midpoint, and 20% upon delivery. For larger projects, we can arrange monthly payment schedules.

Do you work with international clients? +

Absolutely! We work with clients across 40+ countries and are experienced in managing remote projects across different time zones.
